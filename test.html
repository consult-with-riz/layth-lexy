What We're Building

Four deliverables.
One summer window.

01

HubSpot CRM + School Pipeline

HubSpot free tier configured as the CRM. All 120+ UAE schools loaded from your existing pipeline data. Deal stages, contact records, subscription tracking, and follow-up triggers — so the pipeline manages itself between your check-ins.

ACTIVE · AUGUST
02

School Outreach Email Sequences

Three sequences built in HubSpot: principal, inclusion lead, general admin. Claude drafts personalised emails for each school. You review batches before anything sends. Response status auto-logs and triggers the next step.

ACTIVE · AUGUST
03

Instagram Content Engine

Weekly content signals go in, Claude generates caption, hook, hashtag set, and visual brief for five posts. Output lands in your review queue. You approve and post manually. Copy only — image creation is not included. No auto-publishing.

ACTIVE · JUNE
04

Post-Screening Report Generator

When a screener is completed, Claude reads the result from Firebase and generates a plain-language parent report and a teacher summary. Both produced as downloadable documents the same day — no formatting, no 12 pages.
